Game lore:

An unknown disease spread all across the city, one of its main characteristics was deep sleep which in some case took weeks the only known way to break out of the deep sleep was for the patient to realize they are in dream and try to find there way out. you found yourself in a strange world with strange huge buildings and walls all around you, it took you a while but you realized you are in a dream and you should breakout. from what you knew about the disease is that you should reach somewhere which ends the dream but it isnt guranteed to break out first try, so you better start quick or else you would be stuck forever!

The Terrain:

floating islands representing different dreams, they come in different shapes and sizes each of them with mew mazes and challenges, they contain an artifact that represents the end of the dream yor goal is to reach the artifacts to end the dream , but take care each time you end a dream you either be lucky and breakout or get into another dream, which might be dangerous as the could be unfinished dreams with broken terrian making it more challenging.artifacts are found in the terrain usually looks like this in game (@).

Doors:

Huge heavy looking structures can be best described as meadieval castle doors, its impossible to go through without having the key for it. doors could be found in the terrain and they look like this(I), they usually hide the artifact behind them. make use to open them.

Keys:

They are no normal keys they have a distinct shape making them hard to miss, they are used to open doors and you can spot them if look for the ($) icon in game. all you have to do is move over them to get a key.

Voids:

Voids are blank spaces that are really dangerous falling through them might get you stuck forever, they usually appear within the terrian but they noticeably increase in unfinished dreams making them unstable and extremely dangerous.

Objects:

slightly heavy structures found around the terrain they look like a half barrel this shape makes them extremely helpful to pass voids, pushing them over voids will create a path almost like a bridge. they can only be used as a bridge once, you have to wisely choose where to use them. you should find them looking like this (D) in game.

The Last Dream:

getting to the last Dream might be challenging but here are the characteristics of the dream shall you reach it , you usually have a mirrored character that would mirror all your moves. you could use it to help you get out it can collect keys, open doors and much more. your goal is no different than the other dreams you need to reach the artifact, but this time it will get you out of the dreams.
